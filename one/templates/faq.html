Frequently Asked Questions

What are the operating hours of the library?

  • The library is open 24/7.
  • In case of any temporary closures for maintenance or other reasons, students will be notified via WhatsApp from the library's contact number.

What documents do I need to upload at the time of registration?

  • At the time of registration, you will need to upload the following documents:
    • Photo ID
    • Aadhar Card
    • Screenshot of the transaction receipt

How do I select a time slot for accessing the library?

  • When planning your visit, you have the flexibility to choose from various time slots ranging from 6 to 24 hours.
  • You can select one or multiple shifts, depending on your study schedule:
    • Shift 1 (6 hours): 06:00 AM - 12:00 PM (Price: ₹600)
    • Shift 2 (6 hours): 12:00 PM - 06:00 PM (Price: ₹700)
    • Shift 3 (5 hours): 06:00 PM - 11:00 PM (Price: ₹600)
    • Shift 4 (6 hours): 12:00 AM - 06:00 AM (Price: ₹700)
    • For multiple shifts:
      • 2 batches: ₹1100
      • 3 batches: ₹1300
      • 4 batches: ₹1600

How do I pay for my chosen time slot?

  • Payment for the selected time slot can be made online through our website using secure payment methods.

How do I register for library services?

  • To register yourself, click here to fill out the registration form. A registration fee of 100 rupees is required.

Is there a discount available for students?

  • Currently, there are no special discounts available for students, except under special conditions as determined by the management.

Can I extend my access beyond the initial time slot?

  • Yes, you can extend your access by booking an additional time slot and making the necessary payment.

What happens if I need to cancel my booking?

  • Unfortunately, bookings cannot be cancelled once made. However, if you encounter any issues, please contact the library management for assistance.

Can I bring outside materials into the library?

  • Outside materials such as books or electronic devices are allowed in the library, but please be respectful of others and avoid causing disturbances.

Are there printing or photocopying services available in the library?

  • Yes, we provide printing and photocopying services for a nominal fee. Please inquire at the library counter for more information.

  • While using the library's internet facilities, please adhere to our acceptable use policy. Any misuse or violation may result in suspension of library privileges.
