Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy outlines how Knowledge Nook Library collects, uses, protects, and shares personal information obtained from users of our website and library services.

Collection of Personal Information:

  • We may collect personal information such as name, email address, phone number, and address during the registration process for library services.

Use of Personal Information:

  • We may also use this information to send updates, newsletters, or promotional offers related to library services, unless the user opts out of such communications.
  • Personal information collected is used for the purpose of providing library services, including registration, time slot selection, and communication with students regarding their bookings.

Protection of Personal Information:

  • We are committed to protecting the confidentiality and security of personal information collected from users.
  • Appropriate measures are in place to safeguard against unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction of personal information.

Sharing of Personal Information:

  • Personal information collected from users will not be shared, sold, or rented to third parties, except in the following circumstances.
  • With service providers or business partners who assist us in providing library services, subject to confidentiality agreements.
  • When required by law or legal process to comply with a subpoena, court order, or other legal obligation.

Retention of Personal Information:

  • Personal information will be retained only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this privacy policy or as required by law.

User Control Over Personal Information:

  • Users have the right to access, update, or delete their personal information stored in our database.
  • Requests for access, updates, or deletions can be made by contacting the library management.

Changes to Privacy Policy:

  • We reserve the right to modify or update this privacy policy at any time.
  • If you have any questions, concerns, or complaints regarding this privacy policy or our handling of personal information, please contact us at knowledgenooklibrary@gmail.com.
  • By using our website and services, users acknowledge that they have read, understood, and agree to the terms of this privacy policy.
